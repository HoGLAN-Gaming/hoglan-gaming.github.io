FAQI've got some questions?

How do I get tickets?

Tickets are available from Eventbrite.

How can I contact the organisers?

The best way is via emailing enquiries@hoglan.eu

Is there a minimum age?

Yes you must be 18+ to attend one of our events. Please note that if your lucky enough to look under 25 we will ask you to prove you are over 18.

I’ve been Playing Civilisation for 36 Hours - Where Can I Sleep?

We have a dedicated sleeping area, separate from the main LAN room and gaming bar. We recommend bringing an air mattress / camp bed, sleeping bag and pillow. While attendees are considerate towards the beauty sleep of others we do recommend ear plugs in order to secure the best quality shut-eye.

Please be aware that sleeping space is limited and attendees bringing double airbeds, futons or the hollowed out carcass of a Tauntaun may not have enough room.

I Can’t Survive on LAN Alone - What Will I Eat?

HoGLAN sports a tuck-shop selling a variety of drinks and snacks. A dizzying array of local takeaways will also deliver direct to the venue. HoGLAN also runs scheduled food events including the ‘HoG Roast’. Failing that, HoGLAN is situated smack bang in the centre of Sheffield, with plenty of supermarkets, restaurants and bars within walking (or staggering) distance. Eating fellow attendees is discouraged.

I Smell Of Win – Where Can I Wash?

There is a free shower available for use at the venue. Don't forget to bring a towel.

I’m Bored Of Video Games – What Else Is There To Do?

Lots! Between the pub quizzes, movie nights, meals out, board games, and nerf wars we sometimes forget to get any gaming done! For more information on what we’ve got planned for the next LAN, please consult the activities page.

I’ve Pimped My Rig – What About Security?

Entrance to the venue is through a single route that is clearly visible to all attendees. The venue has CCTV throughout and the external doors are locked at night. While their is always someone awake to supervise all equipment is left at the owners risk.

Please note that equipment is left at your own risk, and that the organisers cannot be held responsible for any loss or damage. Securing your equipment with Kensington or similar locks is recommended if safety is a concern to you.

What transport options are there?

The location is situated only 5 minutes walk for Sheffield train station and only 7 minutes from the bus and coach station.

Public Transport Smells of Wee – Is There Parking For My Marvellous Car?

There is currently no parking available at the venue, however there are a number of car parks in the local area that you can use.

Please note that due to the city centre location, parking may be limited on business days.

I can no longer attend, can I get a refund?

Yes refunds can be provided minus a £10 admin fee please email accounts@hoglan.eu if you need to cancel your ticket. Please note that we do not offer any refunds 30 days before the event.

Is my ticket transferable?

We can not presently offer ticket transfers.
