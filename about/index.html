AboutWhat's this thing all about?

HoGLAN is a new social LAN Party, running twice a year around the Easter and Halloween weekends. With a focus on the social side of gaming, this event aims to deliver a more relaxed take on the traditional hypercompetitive LAN party.

Whether enjoying a pint with friends in the gaming bar, making new ones at the munchkin table or firing nerf darts at the organisers, we can guarantee a unique experience that will leave you wanting more.

Tickets are available from Eventbrite!
