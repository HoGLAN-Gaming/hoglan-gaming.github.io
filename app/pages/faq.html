FAQI've got some questions?

How do I get tickets?

Tickets will be available soon from this site!

I’ve been Playing Civilisation for 36 Hours - Where Can I Sleep?

We have a dedicated sleeping area, separate from the main LAN room and gaming bar. We recommend bringing an air mattress / camp bed, sleeping bag and pillow – or fighting over one of the sofas! While attendees are very considerate towards the beauty sleep of others we do recommend ear plugs in order to secure the best quality shut-eye.

Please be aware that sleeping space is limited and attendees bringing double airbeds may not have enough room.

I Can’t Survive on LAN Alone - What Will I Eat?

HoGLAN sports a tuck-shop selling a variety of drinks and snacks. A dizzying array of local takeaways will also deliver to the venue. HoGLAN also often runs scheduled food events including the ‘HoG Hog Roast’. Failing that, HoGLAN is situated a stone’s throw away from the centre of Sheffield, with plenty of supermarkets, restaurants and bars within walking (or staggering) distance. Eating fellow attendees is discouraged.

I Smell Of Win – Where Can I Wash?

There is a free shower available for use at the venue. Along with an organised trip to a local leisure centre for a swim and shower.

I’m Bored Of Video Games – What Else Is There To Do?

Lots! Between the pub quizzes, movie nights, meals out, swimming, board games, and nerf wars we sometimes forget to get any gaming done! For more information please consult the activities.

I’ve Pimped My Rig – What About Security?

Entrance to the venue is through a single route that is clearly visible to all attendees. The venue has CCTV throughout, the external doors are locked from the outside at night. The organisers do their best to ensure someone is always awake to keep an eye on the equipment.

Your equipment is left at your own risk, the organisers can not be held responsible for any loss or damage.

Public Transport Smells of Wee – Is There Parking For My Marvellous Car?

Yes there is, however please let the organisers know ahead of time if you will be bringing one so they can ensure that enough spaces are available.

Please note parking may be limited on business days
