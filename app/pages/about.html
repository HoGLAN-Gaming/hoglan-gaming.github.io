AboutWhat is this thing?

HoGLAN is a private LAN Party being run in Sheffield from Thursday 31st October – Sunday 3rd November. We are a group of gamers that play a wide variety of games. These games include but are definitely not limited to League of Legends, Supreme Commander 2, Natural Selection 2, Civilisation 5, Armagetron, Artemis, World of Warcraft, Eve Online and many more.

We are asking for a contribution of £40 per day from any attending HoG member towards the cost of running the event.
